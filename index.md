## Who is it?

Hi, I'm Tim Pepper, a technologist and armchair philosopher who fancies himself a writer. I've worked as a software developer and general tech person for around a decade but was originally a philosophy graduate. Recently I've become interested in diversity and inclusion in tech and marrying software development with philosophy.

## What is it?

You can find my ramblings in the [blog](blog/) or the different themes in [categories](categories/). For bitesize diversity and inclusion posts you can look at some [TIDbits](categories/#tidbit) or if you're interested in the Philosophy of Software look [here](categories/#philosophy-of-software-engineering). There's something for everyone (who likes diversity or philosophy).
